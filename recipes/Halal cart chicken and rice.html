Halal Cart Chicken and Rice

This is a great recipe for the holidays; it tastes great and it feels like fall when you drink it.
It is similar to the Starbucks® Pumpkin Spice Latte, but I personally like this better. Top
with whipped cream and pumpkin pie spice.

    Ingredients

  • ¼ cup light olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 ½ tablespoons coarsely chopped garlic
  • ½ teaspoon ground coriander
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 pounds boneless chicken thighs, trimmed of excess fat

    Yogurt Sauce:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce, or more to taste
  • 1 ½ tablespoons coarsely chopped garlic
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

    Rice:

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 ½ cups long-grain rice
  • 2 ½ cups chicken broth

    Directions

  1. Step 1

    Combine olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, and coriander in a blender;
    blend until smooth. Season with salt and pepper..

  2. Step 2

    Place chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag; pour in blended marinade
    and seal.
    Refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours.

  3. Step 3

    Whisk yogurt, sriracha sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper together to make the
    sauce. Refrigerate for 1 hour.

  4. Step 4

    Preheat a skillet over medium-high heat. Remove chicken from the marinade and
    pat dry with paper towels. Cook skin-side down until well browned, 4 to 5 minutes.
    Flip and cook until no longer pink in the center, 4 to 5 minutes more.

  5. Step 5

    Let chicken for 5 to 10 minutes, then chop into chunks.

  6. Step 6

    Melt butter in a large Dutch oven over medium heat. Add turmeric and cumin;
    cook and stir until fragrant.Add rice and stir to coat. Cook, stirring frequently,
    until toasted, about 4 minutes. Pour in chicken broth and season with salt and pepper.
    Bring to a boil; reduce heat, cover, and cook until broth is absorbed, about 15 minutes.

  7. Step 7

    Remove Dutch oven from the heat and let stand, covered, for 15 minutes.

  8. Step 8

    Divide rice and chicken among serving bowls. Drizzle yogurt sauce on top.
